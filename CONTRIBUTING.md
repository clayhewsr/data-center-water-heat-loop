# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in this project.

## Goals

Contributions should support the project's objectives of:

- Improving cooling efficiency
- Reducing water consumption
- Recovering thermal energy
- Enhancing operational reliability
- Expanding engineering documentation
- Improving diagrams and visualizations

---

## Ways to Contribute

- Improve documentation
- Report issues
- Suggest design improvements
- Expand engineering calculations
- Improve Mermaid diagrams
- Create SVG engineering illustrations
- Review technical content

---

## Documentation Standards

Please:

- Use clear technical language.
- Keep diagrams simple and readable.
- Document engineering assumptions.
- Maintain consistent formatting.

---

## Future Contributions

Future releases may include:

- Simulation examples
- Software prototypes
- Digital Twin demonstrations
- AI-assisted optimization examples
- Additional engineering references

Thank you for helping improve this engineering concept.
