# Operations Center

## Purpose

The Operations Center serves as the primary interface for monitoring and managing the Data Center Water & Heat Loop Ecosystem.

It brings together operational data from water systems, cooling infrastructure, heat recovery equipment, AI optimization, and supporting facility systems into a unified operator view.

---

# Primary Dashboard

The Operations Center may display:

- Overall system health
- Water system status
- Cooling system status
- Heat recovery status
- Energy consumption
- Thermal storage
- Equipment availability
- Active alerts

---

# Water Management

Operators can monitor:

- Water supply
- Water quality
- Tank levels
- Flow rates
- Pump operation
- Valve status
- Leak detection

---

# Cooling Management

Displays may include:

- Return temperatures
- Rack cooling status
- Heat exchanger performance
- CDU status
- Cooling efficiency

# Heat Recovery

Operators may monitor:

- Distribution efficiency

# Alerts

The dashboard may classify events as:

## Information

Routine operational updates.

## Advisory

Conditions that should be monitored.

## Warning

Conditions requiring operator attention.

## Critical

Conditions requiring immediate response.
