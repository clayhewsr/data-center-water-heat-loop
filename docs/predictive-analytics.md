# Predictive Analytics

## Purpose

The Predictive Analytics layer analyzes historical and current operational data to support forecasting, maintenance planning, and system optimization across the Data Center Water & Heat Loop Ecosystem.

---

# Objectives

- Improve reliability
- Reduce downtime
- Optimize water usage
- Improve cooling efficiency
- Increase heat recovery
- Support long-term planning

---

# Forecasting

The platform may estimate future:

- Cooling demand
- Water demand
- Energy consumption
- Thermal storage utilization
- Heat recovery opportunities
- Equipment loading

---

# Predictive Maintenance

Historical operating data may be used to identify trends that suggest maintenance should be scheduled before equipment performance degrades.

Potential equipment includes:

- Pumps
- Valves
- Heat exchangers
- Cooling distribution units
- Storage tanks
- Monitoring sensors

---

# Trend Analysis

Operational trends may include:

- Water usage
- Energy usage
- Equipment utilization
- Temperature changes
- Flow rates
- Pressure changes
- Heat recovery efficiency

---

# Decision Support

Analytics may assist operators by:

- Identifying abnormal conditions
- Recommending maintenance windows
- Highlighting efficiency opportunities
- Supporting capacity planning
- Improving operational awareness

---

# Future Integration

Future versions may incorporate forecasting models and AI-assisted analysis to improve operational planning while keeping human operators in control of key decisions.
