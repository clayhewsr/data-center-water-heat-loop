# Heat Recovery System

## Purpose

The Heat Recovery System captures thermal energy produced by AI and high-performance computing equipment and redirects it to useful applications instead of rejecting it to the atmosphere.

---

# Heat Sources

- AI GPU clusters
- CPU servers
- Networking equipment
- Power distribution equipment
- Cooling loops
- Heat exchangers

---

# Heat Capture

Heat is collected using:

- Direct liquid cooling
- Plate heat exchangers
- Secondary water loops
- Thermal storage systems

---

# Heat Reuse Applications

## Agriculture

- Greenhouses
- Controlled environment farming
- Hydroponics

---

## Aquaculture

- Fish farming
- Water temperature stabilization
- Hatchery support

---

## Community Heating

- District heating
- Municipal buildings
- Schools
- Hospitals

---

## Industrial Processes

- Process heating
- Drying systems
- Manufacturing support

---

# Thermal Storage

Energy may be stored using:

- Hot water tanks
- Phase-change materials
- Thermal batteries

---

# Monitoring

The system continuously measures:

- Supply temperature
- Return temperature
- Heat transfer efficiency
- Flow rate
- Thermal storage capacity
- Energy recovered

---

# AI Optimization

Artificial intelligence can optimize:

- Pump speeds
- Heat routing
- Thermal storage charging
- Energy demand forecasting
- Predictive maintenance
- Overall system efficiency

---

# Design Goals

- Maximize recovered energy
- Reduce wasted heat
- Improve sustainability
- Lower operating costs
- Increase infrastructure efficiency
