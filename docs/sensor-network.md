# Sensor Network

## Purpose

The Sensor Network provides real-time measurements throughout the Data Center Water & Heat Loop Ecosystem. These measurements support monitoring, automation, predictive maintenance, and operational decision-making.

---

# Water Sensors

The water system may monitor:

- Water temperature
- Flow rate
- Pressure
- Water quality
- Conductivity
- pH
- Tank levels
- Leak detection

---

# Cooling Sensors

Cooling infrastructure may monitor:

- Supply temperature
- Return temperature
- Heat exchanger efficiency
- CDU performance
- Rack inlet temperature
- Rack outlet temperature

---

# Energy Sensors

Energy monitoring may include:

- Electrical power consumption
- Pump power
- Cooling equipment power
- Heat recovery output
- Thermal storage status

---

# Infrastructure Sensors

Infrastructure monitoring may include:

- CPU utilization
- GPU utilization
- Memory utilization
- Storage utilization
- Network traffic
- Server temperatures
- Equipment uptime

---

# Environmental Sensors

Facility monitoring may include:

- Ambient temperature
- Humidity
- Air pressure
- Water intrusion
- Smoke detection
- Vibration monitoring

---

# Data Collection

Sensor information supports:

- Live dashboards
- Historical analysis
- Predictive maintenance
- Capacity planning
- Fault detection
- AI-assisted optimization

---

# Design Principles

The sensor network is designed to provide:

- High reliability
- Continuous monitoring
- Modular expansion
- Standardized interfaces
- Secure data collection
- Scalable deployment
