# Operations Dashboard

## Purpose

The Operations Dashboard provides a centralized interface for monitoring and managing the Data Center Water & Heat Loop Ecosystem.

It combines infrastructure status, water systems, cooling systems, heat recovery, energy management, and operational analytics into a unified view for operators.

---

# System Summary

The dashboard provides a high-level overview of:

- Overall system health
- Water system status
- Cooling system status
- Heat recovery performance
- Energy management
- Infrastructure availability

---

# Water Dashboard

Displays may include:

- Tank levels
- Water quality
- Flow rates
- Pressure
- Pump status
- Valve status
- Leak detection

---

# Cooling Dashboard

Displays may include:

- Supply temperature
- Return temperature
- Cooling capacity
- Rack temperatures
- CDU status
- Heat exchanger performance

---

# Heat Recovery Dashboard

Displays may include:

- Heat captured
- Heat delivered
- Thermal storage
- Distribution status
- Recovery efficiency

---

# Infrastructure Dashboard

Displays may include:

- AI server status
- GPU utilization
- CPU utilization
- Memory utilization
- Storage utilization
- Network activity
- Uptime

---

# Energy Dashboard

Displays may include:

- Electrical demand
- Pump energy
- Cooling energy
- Heat recovery output
- System efficiency
- Historical trends

---

# Alert Center

The dashboard may categorize alerts as:

🟢 Normal

🟡 Advisory

🟠 Warning

🔴 Critical

Each alert should include:

- Time
- System
- Severity
- Recommended action
