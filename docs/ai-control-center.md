# AI Control Center

## Purpose

The AI Control Center acts as the monitoring and optimization layer for the Data Center Water & Heat Loop Ecosystem.

It helps coordinate cooling, water reuse, heat recovery, energy demand, equipment health, and system alerts.

---

# Core Functions

## Sensor Monitoring

The system can track:

- Water temperature
- Water flow
- Pump status
- Valve position
- Pressure
- Heat exchanger performance
- Tank levels
- Cooling demand
- Energy consumption

---

## Optimization

AI can help optimize:

- Pump speeds
- Cooling distribution
- Heat recovery routing
- Thermal storage use
- Maintenance scheduling
- Energy cost reduction
- Water reuse balancing

---

## Predictive Maintenance

The system can look for early signs of:

- Pump wear
- Valve failure
- Heat exchanger fouling
- Leak risk
- Sensor drift
- Cooling inefficiency

---

## Green / Yellow / Red Status

The control layer can classify system health as:

- Green: normal operation
- Yellow: warning condition
- Red: critical condition requiring action

---

# Delta Node Monitor Integration

The Delta Node Monitor can serve as an operational dashboard for tracking compute-node health and infrastructure conditions.

Possible monitored categories include:

- CPU load
- GPU load
- Memory usage
- Network traffic
- Temperature
- Uptime
- Cooling demand
- System health score
