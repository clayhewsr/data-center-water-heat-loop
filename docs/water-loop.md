# Water Loop Design

## Purpose

The closed-loop water system provides reliable cooling for AI and high-performance computing infrastructure while minimizing water waste and maximizing thermal efficiency.

---

# Water Flow

1. Water Source
2. Treatment System
3. Cold Water Storage
4. Pump Station
5. Cooling Distribution Units
6. Server Liquid Cooling
7. Heat Exchangers
8. Warm Water Return
9. Heat Recovery
10. Water Reconditioning
11. Return to Storage

---

# Major Components

## Water Sources

- Municipal water
- Reclaimed water
- Rainwater (optional)
- Emergency reserve

---

## Treatment

- Sediment filtration
- Carbon filtration
- UV sterilization
- Water quality monitoring

---

## Pumping System

- Variable-speed pumps
- Backup pumps
- Pressure sensors
- Flow sensors

---

## Cooling Network

- Supply headers
- Return headers
- Cooling Distribution Units (CDUs)
- Direct-to-chip liquid cooling
- Rack manifolds

---

## Heat Exchangers

- Plate heat exchangers
- Shell-and-tube exchangers
- Dry coolers
- Cooling towers (where appropriate)

---

## Water Monitoring

Continuously monitor:

- Flow rate
- Pressure
- Temperature
- Water quality
- Pump status
- Valve position
- Leak detection
